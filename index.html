About the Vanguard

We are a private fellowship of Orthodox Christian founders, operators, and investors committed to stewardship, courage, and mastery. Our framework aligns business execution with sacramental life, discipline, and duty to family and Church.

Membership is selective. Every man is expected to train his body, sharpen his vocation, and cultivate virtue. Through counsel, referrals, and strategic alliances, members build enduring enterprises that serve the Kingdom with excellence.

2.0 • COMPONENTS

The Three Pillars

Physical Capacity

Train like a protector. Members follow measurable standards in strength, endurance, and readiness to lead under pressure.

Spiritual Fortitude

Live from prayer and repentance. Brotherhood is grounded in Orthodox tradition, confession, liturgical rhythm, and accountability.

Financial Prowess

Build resilient companies. Exchange deal flow, strategy, and operator intelligence to multiply impact and legacy.
